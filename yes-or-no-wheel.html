Yes or No Wheel FAQ

Is the Yes or No Wheel random?

Yes. Each spin randomly lands on one of the loaded options in your browser.

Why only Yes and No?

The default wheel is deliberately simple. You can add Maybe or Try Again if you want a less binary result.

Can I use this for serious decisions?

No. It is intended for fun and low-risk everyday choices, not serious financial, health or legal decisions.

Can I change the answers?

Yes. Edit the entries and load the wheel again.

Simple binary decisions

The Yes or No Wheel is intentionally different from a full multi-option spinner. It is designed for quick binary decisions, so the wheel uses two bold outcomes rather than lots of colours and choices. Ask a question, spin, and get a clear answer.

When yes/no is enough

Use this page for light decisions: should we go out, should we order food, should we watch another episode, should I do the task now? If you have more than two real options, use the Decision Wheel instead.
