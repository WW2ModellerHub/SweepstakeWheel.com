Suggested office sweepstake rules

Keep the rules simple. One team per person, random draw only, and agree the prize split before the draw starts. A common approach is to donate part of the pot to charity and split the rest between the winner and runner-up.

  • One randomly drawn team per person.
  • No swapping teams after the draw.
  • Winner is the person with the tournament-winning country.
  • Runner-up is the person with the losing finalist.

Suggested prize split for a charity sweepstake

A simple split is 50% to charity, 35% to the winner and 15% to the runner-up. For 48 people at £5 each, that creates a £240 pot: £120 to charity, £84 to the winner and £36 to the runner-up.
