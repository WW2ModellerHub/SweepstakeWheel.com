How to run a World Cup sweepstake

Add one person per line, check the country list, load the draw and spin the wheel. The tool removes each country after it has been drawn so every result is unique.

  • Add names in the setup box.
  • Use Shuffle People if you want a random order.
  • Spin manually or use Auto Draw Everyone.
  • Copy the free summary, or unlock the formatted CSV if you need a cleaner spreadsheet-ready version.

Group size guide

You do not need exactly 48 people. If you have fewer people than countries, only the entered names get drawn and the spare countries are left unused. If you have more people than countries, split people into teams or run more than one draw.

  • 12 people: each person can receive one country and many countries remain undrawn.
  • 24 people: one country each works well.
  • 48 people: every country can be assigned.

World Cup sweepstake FAQ

Can I use this for an office World Cup sweepstake?

Yes. It is designed for office sweepstakes, work charity draws and quick team assignments. Add names, load the draw and spin to randomly assign countries.

Can I auto-draw every person?

Yes. Use the Auto Draw Everyone button and the tool will run through the list without you clicking for every person.

Can I copy the results for free?

Yes. After the draw, use Copy Free Summary to copy a plain-text version for Teams, Slack, WhatsApp or email.

What does the CSV upgrade include?

The CSV upgrade gives you a cleaner spreadsheet-ready version with the draw number, name and country in separate columns.

What happens if I have fewer than 48 people?

That is fine. Only the number of people entered will receive countries. The remaining countries simply stay undrawn.

Does the tool store names or results?

No. This version runs in your browser. It does not upload or store your draw data anywhere.

Is this an official FIFA World Cup tool?

No. This is an unofficial fan-made sweepstake tool and is not affiliated with FIFA, the FIFA World Cup or any official tournament organiser.
