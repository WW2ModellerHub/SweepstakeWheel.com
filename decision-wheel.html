Decision Wheel FAQ

What should I put in a decision wheel?

Add the realistic options you would be happy to accept, such as restaurants, activities, destinations or tasks.

Can the wheel decide for me?

It can make a random choice from your list. It is best for low-risk everyday choices, not serious financial, medical or legal decisions.

Can I use decision templates?

Yes. Use the template buttons for restaurants, holidays, weekend activities and date night ideas.

Can I change the options?

Yes. Edit the text box at any time and load the wheel again.

Decision templates that save time

Best decisions for a wheel

A wheel works best for low-risk choices where every option is acceptable. Do not use it for serious financial, medical or legal decisions. Use it for everyday choices like food, activities, order of play, film night, chores and group plans.
